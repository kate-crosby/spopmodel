age_dist {spopmodel}R Documentation

Age frequency of San Francisco Estuary-based White Sturgeon.

Description

A dataset with 20 ages (0-19) and corresponding frequency (see Details) of White Sturgeon (Acipenser transmontanus).

Format

A data frame with 20 rows and 2 variables:

age

numeric age from 0-19

freq

number (frequency) at each age

Details

Frequency for ages 3-19 proportional to starting abundance of 48,000. Age-1 & age-2 frequency predicted from linear regression. Age-0 frequency estimated using age-specific fecundity and percent of females spawning annually (in this case 15%).

Source

Model development input data (CDFW; Univ. of Idaho).
